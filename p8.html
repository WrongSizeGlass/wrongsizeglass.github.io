What is Your Friendly Neighborhood Raccoon

Its a game where you gets to play as a Raccon in a city.

You gets to explore the city, and jump on top of buildings.

Help fixing and cleaning the city

The problems needs to be simple enough so player can figurer them out, without having to tell them.

Design values

The solutions to the problems needs to be solvable by movement and or grabbing mechanics, resulting in the solution would be intitive to what they know they can do.

Lighthearted chill game, fixing weird problems in the city.

Design pillers

As the main goal of the project was to findout how people would solve problems in a game and in a linear vs open quest apporch.
The player should feel not feel stress which could resolve in a high cognitive load from other than solving the problems.
The problems can be weird allowing for a weird comment about the problem to the player, to gain a cheap grin from them, since their motivation to participate in the test is important, as it is within group test.

My roll

For this project I had a quite fluid role spanding from Quest, Level and Game designer, QA tester, and Programmer.
Since as I was responsible for:
designing and implementing the level layout, block out basic objects and their positions.
The second quest in the park with both solution and the thrid quest at the power plant.
The gameplay mechanics, the sound effects including Play testing, and fixing bugs
Lastly scripted the quest controllers, the savefile write/read json script, UI functionality and more.

Features 3D heatmap

To evalutate the players movement, to see if they vent into problems as an explample where the players fails jumps, the most common paths etc. I developed a 3D heatmap that tracked the players position.
This heatmap was quite educational to analyse, and has helped me with my later designs, as well as giving me good inputs to how to improve the level.
