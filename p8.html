My roles for this project was quest, level and game, QA tester, and programmer, since I was responsible: designing and implementing the level layout, block out basic objects and their positions.
The second quest in the park with both solution and the thrid quest at the power plant.
The gameplay mechanics, the sound effects including Play testing, and fixing bugs
Lastly scripted the quest controllers, the savefile write/read json script, UI functionality and more.
Lighthearted chill game, fixing weird problems in the city.
As the main goal of the project was to findout how people would solve problems in a game and in a linear vs open quest apporch. The player should feel not feel stress which could resolve in a high cognitive load from other than solving the problems. The problems can be weird allowing for a weird comment about the problem to the player, to gain a cheap grin from them, since their motivation to participate in the test is important, as it is within group test.
