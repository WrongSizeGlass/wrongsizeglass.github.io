Design values

Design pillers

The solutions to the problems needs to be solvable by movement and or grabbing mechanics, resulting in the solution would be intitive to what they know they can do.

Lighthearted chill game, fixing weird problems in the city.

As the main goal of the project was to findout how people would solve problems in a game and in a linear vs open quest apporch.
The player should feel not feel stress which could resolve in a high cognitive load from other than solving the problems.
The problems can be weird allowing for a weird comment about the problem to the player, to gain a cheap grin from them, since their motivation to participate in the test is important, as it is within group test.
My roles for this project was quest, level and game, QA tester, and programmer, as I was responsible for:
designing and implementing the level layout, block out basic objects and their positions.
The second quest in the park with both solution and the thrid quest at the power plant.
The gameplay mechanics, the sound effects including Play testing, and fixing bugs

Quest 1

The goal of this level was to teach the player how the movement and running jump mechanics worked, in order to complete this level.
The main obsticals in this level was the height of objects and spaces between the objects.
Player needed to collect all the doughnut at the doughnut shop.

Quest 2

The purpose of this quest was to teach the grabbing mechanic to the player.
This quest is about the fountain in the park has stopped working, and the player needs to find a way to provide water to the fountain. As can be seen on the image to the left, there was designed two ways to solve this quest.

The first way is marked in blue, around the benchs at the lake there are empty buckets the player can grab and go into the lake to fill the bucket, and then the player needs to drop the full bucket in the fountant to complete the quest.

The second way is marked in magenta, on the roof on the toilet there is placed a waterpump, which the player needs to place in the middle of the water pipe which streches from the fountain to the toilet building.
