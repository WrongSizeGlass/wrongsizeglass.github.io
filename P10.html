Requim of Ragnarock

A town exploration game where you need to save trapped spirits and defeat Naglfar to save the world from Ragnarock


I made this prototype for my master thesis; the goal was to analyse the players movement patterns through out the city and see if they have developed a good cognitive map over the level.
Two towns were designed based on two ecological models: The sector model and the multiple nuclei model. Due to simplicity, only the sector model will be shown.

Requiem of Ragnarok is an exploration game that prioritizes seamless navigation and encourages players to build and explore their cognitive maps.

Design values

The quest design should help the player create subconscious landmarks of the town, to help their cognitive map of the town.

An exploration game that prioritizes seamless navigation and encourages players to build and explore their cognitive map.

Design pillers

The town is designed to prioritize seamless navigation and encourage players to build and explore their cognitive map of the town.
By emphasizing the importance of accessible navigation, we aim to create an intuitive and enjoyable experience for players.
The project's goal is to explore the impact of different wayfinding techniques on the player's cognitive map of the town.

Quest structure
The quest structure starts with the player exploring the first block (1-6) to familiarize themselves with the game mechanics and world.
Followed by a tour through most of the town (6-11) with bigger landmarks and simpler routes.
The second part is designed to be a straightforward path forward while still allowing players to explore, with a straight path making it easy to return to the quest.
