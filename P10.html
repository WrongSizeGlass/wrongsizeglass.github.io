Ragnarock was a game prototype I co-developeded for my Master thesis
The goal of the project was to explor peoples wayfinding abilites and cognitiv maps so designing and creating a city which was easy to navigate in was a goal.
The to cities I designed for the project was build around two of the ecological models: sector model and the multiple nuclai.
The general Image grid pattern on the page, will show the multiple Nuclie level in the top grid and the Sector level in the bottom grid.

top down overview

When I design levels I prefer to design for them with player agency in mind, so when I design with traversial in mind I designed it few bindpaths so the mainpaths was easy to reconise, I also design the city where the player are able to create shortcuts through the city, and of course provide them with navigational aid.
I also takes the gameplay mechanics and story intoconsideration when I design, to create a silver lining through the game experiance, which also includes the quest structure in this prototype

The most left images shows task locations in the level, and the right most images shows, a player tracking 3D heartmap of my design, but in short the more dense the colors are the more people have been at that point.

Color code: red is the player is doing the quest from 1 to 6. Green means the player is during the quest from 6 to 11. Lastly the blue shows the last quest where the player needs to defeat a boss down at the harbour.
The white lines shows the primary pathsways between the quests.
The heartmaps have been very educational, to see which paths the players generally takes, where they tend to explore and which areas fewer players explorers.
