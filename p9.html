What is Geo City

Its a game where NPCs adapts to the player character.

Different interactions depended on the player

Its a world where the characters are evolve from 2D primitiv geometry

Negative interactions should not be hurtful to the player, but should be direct enough so the player knows that they are in a negative interaction

Design values

The quest the NPCs gives to the player should be simple enough, as the player should focus on the dialog interactions

Dialog based game, where NPCs will react differently towards the player based on the players character.

Design pillers

The goal behind the project was so see if the players could identify if they experiance intersectional biases, based on the dialog they encountered. The NPC and the player should have distinct dialog depended on what the player and the active NPC looks like, and the NPC should react to the player based on NPC's current perception of the player. Negativ interactions should not be hurtful to the player, thus the characters should not be humannoid, so negativ dialog can be negativ with the player might reflect the comments on themselfs.

Features 3D heatmap

To evalutate the players movement, to see if they vent into problems as an explample where the players fails jumps, the most common paths etc. I developed a 3D heatmap that tracked the players position.
This heatmap was quite educational to analyse, and has helped me with my later designs, as well as giving me good inputs to how to improve the level.
