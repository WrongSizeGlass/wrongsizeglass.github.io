What is Geo City

Its a game where NPCs give different interactions to the player character.

Its a game filled with geometric objects.

An experimental serious game, to see if people can identify intersectional biases

Negative interactions should not be hurtful to the player, but should be direct enough so the player knows that they are in a negative interaction

Design values

The quest the NPCs gives to the player should be simple enough, as the player should focus on the dialog interactions

Dialog based game, where NPCs will react differently towards the player based on the players character.

Design pillers

The goal behind the project was so see if the players could identify if they experiance intersectional biases, based on the dialog they encountered. The NPC and the player should have distinct dialog depended on what the player and the active NPC looks like, and the NPC should react to the player based on NPC's current perception of the player. Negativ interactions should not be hurtful to the player, thus the characters should not be humannoid, so negativ dialog can be negativ with the player might reflect the comments on themselfs.

Reputation system

The image above shows an overview and simplification of the reputation system is effected by the other characters attributes with the self character's biases, as well as the goodwill the character has towards the other character.
These stats are then also effect on what type of dialog the other character says to the self character.
For the NPCs the end value of the goodwill attributes will the dictate what the NPC will respond to the player and if the player will get an reward from the NPC.
The image above repersents a more detailed visualisation of how the reputation system works and which attributes are effected by which attributes.
While there are some flaws with this system in regards to the intersectional inspiration, I have found that the system works fine as an early prototype, for a more complex reputation system.
Based on the world building and the reasoning for different biases, most NPCs generally has good perception towards the characters who belongs to the following visual social groups: single colored, thick, and triangluar prishms.
Invirese the NPCs would generally have a negative perception towards characters who belongs to the following visual social groups: checked, thin, and cubed. The img above shows all teh rewards the player can get if they only takes the polight dialog option, towards the quest given NPCs.
Due to the scope of the project many design elements show casing different types of concecvenses was scraped.
